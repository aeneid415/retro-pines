Vision

Retro Pines Bed and Breakfast envisions itself to become a leading provider of memorable experience through accommodation, by providing excellent service to meet the guest satisfaction and serving mouth watering food and refreshing beverages.

Mission

Retro Pines bed and breakfast commits itself to:

  • Provide high end nostalgic rooms equipped with up to date technology;
  • Offer satisfying food and rejuvenating beverages; and
  • Render quality service that ensures a high level of guest satisfaction.
